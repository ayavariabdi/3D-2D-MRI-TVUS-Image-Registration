This repository contains the Matlab codes used and developed for the article  

Mapping and Characterizing Endometrial Implants by Registering 2D Transvaginal Ultrasound to 3D Pelvic Magnetic Resonance Images
A. Yavariabdi, A. Bartoli, C. Samir, M. Artigues, D. Da Ines and M. Canis
Journal of Computerized Medical Imaging and Graphics, 45:11-25, July 2015 

When using the codes or datasets in this repository in your work, please cite the above paper.

For more information or questions, please e-mail amir.yavariabdi@gmail.com or adrien.bartoli@gmail.com.
